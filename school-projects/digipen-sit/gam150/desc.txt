Game Description: 
2D top-down, 2-player local multiplayer escape room game. Made using Alpha Engine Framework.

Read More:
Players take on the roles of two cats, Bob and Cob, test subjects at a research facility that have obtained hyper-intelligence. Refer to the task list and complete co-op mini-games around the map! Avoid getting caught by enemies. Break your partner out of jail by completing the jail minigame. Break objects in the facility to gain bonus points.
Win Condition:
Complete all tasks on the task list and head to the exit (brown doors)
Lose Condition:
Both players get caught or total player lives go down to 0

My contributions:
Procedural Map Generation using Binary Space Partitioning
