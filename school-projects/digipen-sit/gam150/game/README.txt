# OVERVIEW

What is Bob and Cob?

Catastrophe is a 2D top-down, 2-player local multiplayer escape room game. Players take on the roles of two cats, Bob and Cob, test subjects at a research facility that have obtained hyper-intelligence.

Gameplay elements:

Refer to the task list and complete co-op mini-games around the map! Avoid getting caught by enemies.

Break your partner out of jail by completing the jail minigame.

Break objects in the facility to gain bonus points.

Win Condition:

Complete all tasks on the task list and head to the exit (brown doors)

Lose Condition:

Both players get caught or total player lives go down to 0

================================================================
## Controls
 
Player 1 controls
WASD keys for movement
E key to interact with surroundings.
Q key to exit mini-game

Player 2 controls
Arrow keys for movement
Comma key to interact with surroundings.
Dot key to exit mini-game


Pause overlay: Esc key 

================================================================

## Cheat key presses
F1 - Alternate mashing minigame
F2 - Alternate keypress minigame
F3 - Keypad minigame
