Game Description: 
A virtual reality, real-time multiplayer, account-based, personalised training system.
Made using Unity Engine for MSC Maritime Digital Challenge 2021 Kanda Challenge Statement(Awarded Champion) https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2jRoh6NGUMA

Read More(Game Concept):
Perform procedures onboard the LNG ship, experience random hazard events while performing tasks.
Genre: Serious Gaming, VR Simulation
Multiplayer(Cooperative Play): 1-3 players
Platform: Oculus Quest 2
Target Audience: Seafarers on LNG vessel operating the FGSS
Unique Selling Points: User Profile, Grading System, Refresher Courses
