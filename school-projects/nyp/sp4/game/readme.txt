2D top down roguelike dungeon crawler with singleplayer and multiplayer(maximum 4 players) modes, 4 different characters(rogue, barbarian, fighter, wizard).

The land has been taken over by the Dark Lord of the dragons, Emmapurror. It is up to you to stop her. Travel the maze to reach the end and rejoice alone or with teammates.

Theme: Medieval, Fantasy, Has Dragons
Engine: Unity2D (version 2020.1.7f)
Resolution: Fullscreen 1920x1080 (16:9)
Platforms: PC & Mobile
Unique Selling Point: Maze generation for replayability


On PC platform:
Movement 				- WASD
Range Attack				- Right mouse button
(Singleplayer) Melee Attack   		- Left mouse button
Opening Chests			        - E
Pick Up Weapons 			- F
Open Inventory				- I
Special Abilities 			- Space


On Mobile platform: (single player only)
Movement				- Joystick
Range Attack				- Skill 1 button
Melee Attack				- Attack button
Open inventory				- Pause button


Items:
Attack pills/powders : increases the attack temporarily 
Ironskin pills/powders : increases the defense temporarily
Health potion/powders : recovers health
Mana potion/powders : recovers mana 
Energy bar/potion/ powders : reduces the cooldown of abilities 
Food : heals the player by a small amount 

Weapons:
Battleaxe : used by the barbarian
Wands : used by the wizard
Spears : used by the fighter
Throwing knife : used by the rogue

Characters: 
Rogue: has melee and ranged attack, special ability “stealth” to hide from enemies
Barbarian: has melee and ranged attack, special ability push back enemies
Fighter: has melee and ranged attack, special ability deal critical damage to enemies
Wizard: has melee and ranged attack

Enemies:
Dragons: has ranged attack, will chase after the player to try to kill the player.
Dragonborn: has melee attack, will chase after the player to try to kill the player.

Loot boxes:
Loot boxes will drop from enemies and can be interacted with using the E key. Once the player has interacted with the loot box, it will open and drop a bunch of weapons and items for the player’s use.

Dungeon:
The dungeon is procedurally generated and will be random and unique for every play through. So that the player will not experience the same dungeon twice!

Shop:
Players have a chance to earn money in the dungeon. They can later spend that money in the shop that is located in the hub world. 
Each item has their own values. Items are randomized in the shop. After the players buy an item it will be sent immediately into their inventory and they can use the item there.

Inventory:
Our player’s Inventory is accessed by pressing the I key. Each player has 15 inventory slots. Each slot can hold one of each item
The player can rearrange the items by left clicking on an item and left clicking another slot to place it there
The player can use items by right clicking items in the inventory. Each item used will confer a temporary effect which will boost player stats
Player can also discard items by clicking on the item then click on the trashcan in the inventory

Combo System:
It is there to give the players a competitive feature and to give them a sense of pride and accomplishment.

Online Lobby:
Players can set their username in the lobby before joining / entering a room.
Creating a room without entering a name will generate a random name e.g.(Room3245).
Players can click on the character buttons to select their character and click on the ready/reselect button to set status to ready or reselect a character.
Only the lobby leader can start the game when all players are ready.
